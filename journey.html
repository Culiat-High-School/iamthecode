Orientation Phase

The orientation session gathered students, and teachers to provide a clear overview of the program. Key objectives, expectations, and timelines were explained, helping all participants gain a well-defined understanding of what lies ahead.

Learning Phase
During the learning phase, participants worked through and completed 12 structured modules designed to build their skills step by step. Each module introduced key concepts and practical exercises, allowing learners to strengthen their understanding through consistent practice. By progressing through all twelve modules, participants developed a solid foundation and gained the confidence needed to move forward in the program.
Creation of Website Phase
The Creation of Website Phase focused on applying learned concepts to design and build a functional website. Participants translated their knowledge from the completed modules into practical development tasks, including layout structuring, content organization, and basic styling. This phase emphasized hands-on creation, problem-solving, and attention to detail, allowing learners to transform ideas into a working digital output.
