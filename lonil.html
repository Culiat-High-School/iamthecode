Experience

Borrowing Mini Laptops

This photo was taken during the time when we were allowed to borrow mini laptops provided by the school. These devices played an important role in helping us begin our journey with the modules of IamtheCode. Having access to these mini laptops made our learning more convenient and hands-on, since we were able to follow the modules directly, practice activities, and explore new concepts in coding and technology. This experience marked the starting point of our growth in digital skills.

Helping Each Other

This is where we helped each other while working on the IamtheCode module by sharing ideas, assisting classmates who encountered difficulties, and explaining steps that may be confusing. Through teamwork and cooperation, we were able to complete tasks more efficiently and better understand the lessons, especially in coding activities that require problem solving and focus. This experience highlights the importance of collaboration in developing our technology skills.

Setting Up the Club Booth

We built up a booth representing the IamtheCode club where we worked together to prepare and organize the materials, decorate the area, and present our club to other students. This activity allowed us to showcase the purpose of IamtheCode, promote interest in coding and technology, and strengthen teamwork among members. Through setting up the booth, we learned how to cooperate, communicate effectively, and take responsibility while building confidence in representing our club.
