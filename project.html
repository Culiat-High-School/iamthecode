Our Projects

Our Projects at iamtheCODE empower learners and the community. From our Anti-Bullying Program to free entrance exam reviews and modules, and community mapping initiatives, each project combines learning, service, and real-world impact.

Anti Bullying Campaign

The Anti-Bullying Program promotes respect, empathy, and inclusion, helping students stand against bullying and discrimination.

Free Review & Modules

iamtheCODE's Free Modules provide accessible learning resources to help students strengthen their skills and prepare for entrance exams.

Community Mapping

The Culiat High School with iamthecode community brought education closer to everyone through a community mapping initiative, ensuring no learner—regular, balik-aral, ALS enrollee, or transferee—is left behind.

Anti Bullying Campaign

"The B.A.D. Campaign ('Bullying and Discrimination — Bad Yan') encourages students to take a stand against bullying and discrimination. Through iamtheCODE's participation, the program promotes respect, empathy, and inclusion, inspiring everyone to create a safe and supportive community where kindness and understanding thrive."
