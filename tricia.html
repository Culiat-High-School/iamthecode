Accomplishments

Completing the HTML Course

The first achievement I received was completing the HTML course. Doing the lab activities greatly helped me to further comprehend the course. I realized that putting what you've learned into practice is a way to fully grasp it. I considered this an achievement because I truly struggled to understand the learning videos at first.

Finishing Modules in One Month

My second achievement was being able to finish almost all of the modules in one month. I never really thought I would be able to finish them because I am not that into coding. My commitment and perseverance were the main reasons behind my completion. I realized that as long as you trust the process, you will be able to find the beauty of it, even if it's not something you initially enjoy.

Gaining New Skills

My third achievement was being able to gain new knowledge throughout this journey and becoming capable of making a website. I'm very grateful to all of the people who helped me along the way. In the end, I realized that sometimes, venturing into things you're not familiar with can further sharpen your skills and help you improve yourself.
