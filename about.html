iamtheCODE Goals

  • Empower young people through digital education and technology.
  • Bridge the digital divide in marginalised communities.
  • Promote STEAMD for social and economic transformation.
  • Build inclusive global innovation networks.
  • Encourage ethical and human-centred technology use.
  • Inspire future creators of technology.

iamtheCODE Values

Generosity

We deeply value and appreciate young girls, foster inclusive and diverse communities, support one another through genuine listening, and uplift people by offering opportunities, challenging growth, and giving selflessly while paying it forward.

Compassion

We are dedicated to developing future leaders through respectful, compassionate action, supporting one another without judgment, acting as a force for good, and taking responsibility by apologizing when we fall short.

Creativity

We exist to create collective impact by amplifying marginalized voices, using our talents to share their stories, and standing for a purpose greater than ourselves.

Authenticity

We act with authenticity and humility, openly participating, learning from others, giving proper credit, and sharing knowledge to strengthen the community.

Respect

At iamtheCODE, respect is mutual—shown through respectful communication, trust, accountability, honoring commitments, and valuing one another as leaders.

Impact

Together we are stronger. We share a common purpose, which is to enable one million women and girls coders by 2030. We take personal leadership and responsibility to act against all odds. We are purposedriven and strive to elevate humanity no matter what barriers stand in the way of progress. We do not give up. We keep moving.

Collaboration

We collaborate on impactful projects, value diverse perspectives to drive innovation for young girls, and consistently provide feedback to strengthen iamtheCODE programs.

Listening

We practice respectful, open listening by valuing every voice, avoiding judgment, and fostering inclusive dialogue through moderation and mediation.

Humility

We nurture passionate leaders joining our Foundation and empower high achievers. We believe in learning, transferring knowledge, and paying it forward. We understand that we are not always right and that we do not have all the answers. We surround ourselves with people who know better than us. We strive to do better so we can better in serving our young girls.

Loyalty

People are our greatest asset, we invest in, support, and reward them so they can give their best in empowering young women and girls and bridging the mission to those we serve.

Co-creation

We embrace co-creation, sharing and improving ideas collaboratively, working with our people to design solutions for positive change and social impact.

Kindness

At iamtheCODE, kindness goes beyond being nice it is expressed through empathy, compassion, acceptance, thoughtful actions, and understanding, including toward ourselves when we make mistakes.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is iamtheCODE at Culiat High School?
+
iamtheCODE is part of a global movement offering coding and digital skills training to students. Through DepEd's partnership with the organization, selected public schools like Culiat High School can participate in a program that provides access to a 12-week blended curriculum and thousands of free online courses focused on STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, Mathematics, Design) and technical skills.
2. Who can join the iamtheCODE program at Culiat High School?
+
The initiative typically targets Junior High and Senior High School students of all genders, with particular support for young women and girls to build confidence in tech fields. Participation details are shared by the school once the program is available locally.
3. What kind of skills will students learn in iamtheCODE?
+
Students may learn foundational coding concepts, problem-solving skills, AI basics, and other digital competencies through both online and workshop-style lessons. The program integrates both technical and soft skills training (e.g., teamwork, logical thinking).
4. How long is the iamtheCODE program at Culiat High School?
+
The standard curriculum provided by iamtheCODE is designed as a 12-week blended course, which mixes self-paced online modules with guided sessions, depending on how the school implements it through DepEd's support.
5. Is iamtheCODE free for students at Culiat High School?
+
Yes — the courses and training offered through iamtheCODE are free for participating students, as part of DepEd's initiative to expand access to coding and digital skills across public schools in the Philippines
